Glitterdust
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: 10ft radius
Duration: round/level
Components: V, S, M
A cloud of gold dust blinds everyone in an area and covers them in visible dust. The dust cannot be removed. Creatures that fail their Will save are blinded, but still covered in dust. They get an additional save each round.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: Will partial, Spell Resistance: no
