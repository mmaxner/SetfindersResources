Color Spray
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 15ft Cone
Components: V, S, M
A burst of colours springs from your hand, stunning creatures in its area unless they succeed a Will save. Creatures with up to 2HD are unconcious, blind, and stunned for 2d4 rounds. Up to 4HD are blind and stunned for 1d4 rounds. All creatures are stunned for 1 round. Does no affect sightless creatures.
Range: 15ft cone
Saving Throw: will negates, Spell Resistance: Yes
