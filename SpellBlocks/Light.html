Light
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: Object touched
Duration: 10min/level
Components: V, DF
Cause an object to glow like a torch, providing normal light in a 20ft radius, and increasing light levels for an additional 20ft. You may only have one light at a time, casting a new one cancels your existing light.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
