Shillelagh
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1min/level
Target: non-magical club or staff touched
Components: V, S, DF
Your non-magical club or quarterstaff becomes a +1 weapon that deals damage as if it were 2 sizes bigger (small weapon deals 1d8, medium deals 2d6). This only lasts while the weapon is wielded by the caster.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
