Burning Hands
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 15ft cone
Components: V, S
A cone of searing flame shoots from your fingertips. Any creature in the area takes 1d4 fire damage per caster level, up to 5d4. Flammable materials catch fire in the area.
Range: 15ft
Saving Throw: Reflex halves, Spell Resistance: yes
