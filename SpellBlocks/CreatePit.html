Create Pit
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1round +1/level
Components: V, S, F
An extra dimensional pit, 10x10ft wide and 10ft deep /2 caster levels appears. Must be on a horizontal surface. Creatures in the area get a Reflex save to hop out of the way when created. Creatures adjacent must make saves to avoid falling in. DC 25 Climb to climb out.
Range: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Reflex negates, Spell Resistance: no
