Ghost Sound
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Effect: Illusion of Sounds
Duration: 1 Round/level
Components: V, S, M
Creates a sound that can stay still, rise, approach, or recede. You cannot change the sound after it is created. You can create the volume of 4 humans per caster level. A roaring lion is 16 humans, a dragon is 40.
Range: Close (25ft + 5ft/2 levels)
Saving Throw: Will disbelieves, Spell Resistance: no
