Daze
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: 1 humanoid with <5 HD
Duration: 1 round
Components: V, S, M
Cloud the mind of a weak creature so that it takes no actions. The creature is not stunned, and is immune to further dazing for 1 minute.
Range: Medium(100ft + 10ft/2 levels
Saving Throw: will negates, Spell Resistance: yes
