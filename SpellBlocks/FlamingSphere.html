FlamingSphere
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1 round/level
Components: V, S, M
A flaming sphere appears. It can move up to 30ft if you direct it with a move action. It stops if it hits a creature. It deals 3d6 fire damage (Reflex negates). It can roll over barriers up to 4ft high.
Range: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Reflex negates, Spell Resistance: no
