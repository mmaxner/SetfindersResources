Emergency Force Sphere
4
Casting Time: immediate action
Effect: 5ft radius hemisphere of force
Duration: round/level
Components: V
Create a dome of force around you (hardness 20, 10hp/level). The bottom edge forms a fairly watertight seal on a flat surface. The force cannot move and is imune to dispell mafic. Disintegrate automatically destroys the force. Spells other than teleportation and sight-based spells cannot pass through.
Range: 5ft
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
