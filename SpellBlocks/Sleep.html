Sleep
1
Casting Time: Full Round
Target: creatures in a 10ft radius
Duration: 1 min/level
Components: V, S, M
Up to 4 HD of creatures fall into a magical slumber unless they succeed a Will save. Sleeping creatures are helpless. Noise cannot wake them, but physical contact such as being slapped (a standard action) can.
Range: Medium(100ft + 10ft/2 levels
Saving Throw: Will negates, Spell Resistance: yes
