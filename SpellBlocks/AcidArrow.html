Acid Arrow
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1 round + 1 round/3 levels
Components: V, S, M
Make a ranged touch attack to hit the neemy with an arrow of acid. It deals 2d4 acid damage per round, and lasts for 1 round + 1 per 3 caster levels.
Range: long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: no
