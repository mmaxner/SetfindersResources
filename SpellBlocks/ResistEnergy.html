Resist Energy
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 10minutes/level
Components: V, S, DF
1 creature touched gains energy resistance to your choice of : acid, fire, cold, electric, sonic. This resistance starts at 10, increases to 20 at 7th CL, and 30 at 11th CL.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: none, Spell Resistance: no
