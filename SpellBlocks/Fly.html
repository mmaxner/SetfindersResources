Fly
3
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: creature touched
Duration: minute/level
Components: V, S, F
Subject can fly at 60ft with good manueverability, or 40ft if encumbered by armor. They gain a bonus of 1/2 caster level on fly checks. When the spell ends for any reason (except anti-magic) the subject falls at 60ft/round for 1d6 rounds.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: yes
