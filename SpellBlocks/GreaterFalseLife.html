Greater False Life
4
Casting Time: standard action
Target: you
Duration: hour/level, or until discharged
Components: V, S, M
Gain 2d10 + 1/level temporary hp. Temporary hp is removed before other hp, and cannot be healed. It cannot be stacked (although a new source of temporary hp can replace a smaller amount).
Range: 400ft + 40ft/level
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
