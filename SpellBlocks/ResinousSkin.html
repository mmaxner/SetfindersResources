Resinous Skin
3
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: you
Duration: 10minutes/level
Components: V, S
Coat your body in a resinous substance. You gain DR 5/piercing, and enemies that strike you with a weapon must make a reflex save or have their wepon stick to you. A strength check (DC same as save) can remove the weapon. You get +4 vs disarm, +2 to grapple, +2 vs grapple.
Range: personal
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
