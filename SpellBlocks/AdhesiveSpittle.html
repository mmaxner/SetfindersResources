Adhesive Spittle
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: one creature
Components: V, S
You may spit as a standard action. The adhesive lasts for 2d4 rounds. After being hit, a creature takes -2 to attack rolls, -4 to dex, and moves at half speed until the goo is removed (hp15). If they fail the Reflex save, they are stuck and need a strength check to escape.
Range: 15ft
Saving Throw: Reflex partial, Spell Resistance: no
