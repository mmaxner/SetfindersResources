Alarm
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 20ft radius emanation
Duration: 2 hours/level
Components: V, S, DF
Anytime a Tiny or larger creature passes through the designated area, you are alerted. Choose when you cast the spell: Audible alarm: anyone can hear up to 180ft away, or Mental alarm: a mental ping lets you know about the tresspasser.
Range: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Saving Throw: none, Spell Resistance: no
