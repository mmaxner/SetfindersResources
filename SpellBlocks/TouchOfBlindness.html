Touch of Blindness
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: creature touched/level
Duration: 1 round/level
Components: V
Touch an enemy and their eyes are engulfed in supernatural darkness. They are blinded for 1 round if they fail their save. You may continue to make touch attacks until you have used 1 per level, or the duration of the spell is over, whichever comes first.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: Fort negates, Spell Resistance: yes
