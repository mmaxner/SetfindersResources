Euphoric Cloud
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Effect: 20ft radius, 20ft high vloud
Duration: 1 round/level
Components: V, S, M
Create a bank of fog. Creatures in the cloud must make a Fort save or be Fascinated. The fog blocks vision beyond 5ft, adjacent creatures ahve partial concealment. Creatures stay fascinated for 1d4+1 rounds after the cloud leaves.
Range: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Fort negates, Spell Resistance: no
