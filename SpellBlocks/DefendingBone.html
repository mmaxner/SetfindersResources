Defending Bone
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1 hour/level
Components: V, S, DF
An animated bone floats between you and attacks. The bone grants DR 5/Bludgeoning. After preventing 5 damage/caster level (max 50 damage), it is destroyed. This cannot be used with other forms of DR.
Range: self
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
