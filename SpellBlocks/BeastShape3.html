Beast Shape 3
5
POLYMORPH
Casting Time: standard action
Duration: minute/level
Components: V, S, M
Transform into an animal between Diminutive and Huge size, or a Small or Medium magical beast. Gain up to 90ft of unique speeds, darkvision 60ft, blindsense 30ft, and low-light vision, scent, constrict, ferocity, grab, jet, poison, pounce, rake, trample, trip, and web.
Range: personal
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
