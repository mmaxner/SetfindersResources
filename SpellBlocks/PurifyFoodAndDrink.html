Purify Food and Drink
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: 1 cubic ft./level food and water
Components: V, S
Makes food or water that is spoiled, rotten, diseased, poisonous, or otherwise contaminated safe for eating or drinking. The food or water may spoil again after this spell is cast.
Range: 10ft
Saving Throw: none, Spell Resistance: yes
