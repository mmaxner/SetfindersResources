Ray Of Enfeeblement
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1 round/level
Components: V, S
Make a ranged touch attack to drain the targets strength. The subject takes 1d6 +1/2 caster levels Strength damage, which is halved if they succeed their fortitude save.
Range: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Saving Throw: Fort half, Spell Resistance: yes
