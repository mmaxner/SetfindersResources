Fireball
3
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 20ft radius
Duration: instant
Components: V, S, M
A searing explosion of flame deals 1d6/level (maximum 10d6) to all creatures in area. You fire a pea sized projectile from your finger to the target area. It lights combustibles and melts soft metals like gold and lead.
Range: long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Reflex halves, Spell Resistance: yes
