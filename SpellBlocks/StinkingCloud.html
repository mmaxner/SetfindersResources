Stinking Cloud
3
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 20ft radius, 20ft high
Duration: round/level
Components: V, S, M
Creates a bank of fog that nauseates (nauseated: can only take on move action) creatures inside it, and for 1d4+1 rounds after leaving. Creatures need to make a save when the could appears, and on your turn when it remains in the cloud. It is a poison effect. The fog blocks vision beyond 5ft.
Range: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Fort negates, Spell Resistance: no
