Goodberry
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1day/level
Target: 2d4 fresh berries touched
Components: V, S, DF
Make 2d4 freshly picked berries magical. Druids can always tell if berries are Goodberries. Each berry provides nourishment as if it were an entire meal for a medium creature. The berries heal 1 point of damage, but a creature can't heal more than 8 points per day from Goodberries.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
