Burning Disarm
1
Casting Time: standard action
Target: held metal item
Duration: instant
Components: V, S
The metal objects becomes red hot. The holder may attempt a Reflex save to drop the item and take no damage, otherwise it takes 1d4 damage/caster level (max 5d4). Underwater, this produces steam and deals half damage (no save).
Range: Short (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Saving Throw: Reflex partial, Spell Resistance: no
