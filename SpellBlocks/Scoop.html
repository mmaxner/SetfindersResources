0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Effect: 6 inch diameter container of force
Duration: concentration
Components: V, S
Create a small vessel of force. As a move action, it can be moved up to 15ft/round. Can hold 1pint of liquid or 5 pounds of small objects, and can gather that much from the ground in 1 minute.
Range: Close (25ft + 5ft/2 levels)
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
