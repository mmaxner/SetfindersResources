False Belief
2
Casting Time: 10 minutes + length of memory
Target: willing creature touched
Duration: 1 hour/level
Components: V, S, M
You temporarily alter the target’s memory to eliminate, change, or implant a memory of up to 1 hour in length. When the duration of this expires, the target’s real memory returns, and the false memory fades to little more than a vague outline, like a dream.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: yes
