Feather Fall
1
Casting Time: Immediate action
Target: medium or smaller object/level within 20ft
Duration: round/level, or until landed
Components: V
Creature falls slowly at 60ft per round and takes no falling damage. If the spell ends, falling resumes. Large creatures count as 2, huge as 4, etc...
Range: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Saving Throw: Will (harmless), Spell Resistance: yes
