Disguise Self
At Will
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: you
Components: V, S
Make yourself and your clothes look different. You can appear up to 1 foot taller or shorter, thinner or fatter, as a different subtype, slightly different, or a different gender. If you make a disguise this way, +10 to the diguise. Creatures interacting with you get a will save to disbelieve the illusion.
Range: personal
Saving Throw: Will disbelieves*, Spell Resistance: no
