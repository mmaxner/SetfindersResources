Tail Strike
3
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: you
Duration: min/level
Components: V, M
You grow a draconic tail that matches your bloodline, or your tail extends 5ft if you already have one. It is a secondary natural attack dealing 1d6 at medium, plus half your strength. You can end this as a swift action to roar, making a demoralize attempt on enemies in 30ft.
Range: personal
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
