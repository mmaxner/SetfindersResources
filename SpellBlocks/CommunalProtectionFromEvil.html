Communal Protect from Evil
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: creatures touched
Duration: min/level
Components: V, S, M
Subjects gain +2 deflection bonus and +2 resistance bonus vs evil creatures. Subjects can make a save at +2 to throw off ongoing possession/mind control from evil creatures for the duration of this spell, and are immune to these effects going forward. Evil summouned creatures cannot touch subjects.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
