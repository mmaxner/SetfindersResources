Detect Magic
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 60ft Cone
Duration: concentration
Components: V, S
You detect magical auras in an area. The first round you detect the presence of any uara. The second round detects how many auras are there, and how powerful they are. On the third round, you can identify magic items/spells.
Range: 60ft
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: no
