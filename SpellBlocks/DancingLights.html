Dancing Lights
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1 minute(D)
Components: V, S
You may create up to 4 lanterns, 4 spheres of light, or 1 vaguely humanoid light. These lights can move up to 100feet per round, but cannot be more than 10ft away from each other.
Range: medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: no
