Magic Missile
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Targets: up to 5 creatures within 15ft
Components: V, S
1 missile (+1 per 2 levels past 1st) dart from your fingertip and strike a target for 1d4+1 force damage each. This missile never misses, and can maneuver around cover and concealment. Each missile can have a different target.
Range: medium (100ft + 10ft/level)
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
