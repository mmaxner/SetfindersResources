Produce Flame
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Duration: 1min/level
Effect: Magical Flame in your Palm
Components: V, S
Flames as bright as a torch appear in your palm. You can touch an enemy with these flames, or toss the flames (ranged touch attack) up to 120ft. They deal 1d6 damage, +1 per caster level up to +5. A new flame instantly appears in your hand.
Range: self
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: yes
