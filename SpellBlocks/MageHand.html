Mage Hand
0
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: unattended, non-magic object <5lbs
Duration: concentration
Components: V, S
You point your finger at an object, and can then lift and move it at will. As a move action, you can propel the object up to 15ft in any direction.
Range: Close (25ft + 5ft/2 levels)
Saving Throw: None, Spell Resistance: no
