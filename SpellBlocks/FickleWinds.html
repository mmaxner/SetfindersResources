Fickle Winds
5
Casting Time: standard action
Duration: minute/level
Target: 1 creature/level within 30ft
Components: V, S
A mobile cylinder of wind encompasses each target. It acts a wind wall, except it does not interfere with the target in any way. Small flying creatures, gases, most breath weapons, arrows and bolts cannot pass. Other projectiles have 30% miss chance.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: yes
