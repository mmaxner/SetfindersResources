Trap Rune
4/day
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: 5ft square surface
Duration: 24 hours
Components: V, S
Creates a near invisible rune on a surface. The next creature to touch the rune sets it off, causing an elemental explosion for 1d8+1 damage. A Reflex save halves this.
Range: 15ft
Saving Throw: Reflex partial, Spell Resistance: no
