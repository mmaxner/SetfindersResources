Burst of Radiance
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Area: 10ft radius
Duration: instant
Components: V, S, M
Creatures in the burst that fail their save are Blinded for 1d4 rounds, or dazzled for 1d4 rounds if they succeed. Evil creatures also take 1d4 damage/caster level (max 5d4).
Range: long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
Saving Throw: Reflex partial, Spell Resistance: yes
