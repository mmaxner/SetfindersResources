Charm Person
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: 1 humanoid
Duration: 1 hour/level
Components: V, S
Target humanoid treats you as a trusted friend. If attacking or threatening them, they get +5 to their save. Target never does anything suicidal, and you mut succeed an opposed charisma check to make it do anything it wouldn't normally do. You or an ally threatening the target breaks the charm.
Range: close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Saving Throw: Will negates, Spell Resistance: Yes
