Mirror Image
2
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: you
Duration: min/level
Components: V, S
Create 1d4 + 1/3levels (max 8 total) illusory doubles of yourself. Whenever you are targeted by a successful attack roll, roll randomly to see if you or an image is hit. Figments are destroyed in one hit. Misses by 5 or less also destroy figments. Area spells do not.
Range: personal
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
