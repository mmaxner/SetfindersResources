Keep Watch
1
Casting Time: Standard action
Target: creature touched/2 levels
Duration: 8 hours or less
Components: V, S
Affected creature can stand watch throughout the night without ill effect. They suffer no fatigue and gain the normal benefits of a full night's sleep. Doing any vigorous activity immediately ends the spell. They can begin sleeping or have it recast to resume their rest.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: no, Spell Resistance: no
