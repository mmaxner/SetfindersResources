Enhance Water
1
Casting Time: Full Round
Target: 1 Pint water/level
Components: V, S
Transform water into an alcoholic beverage, typically ale, beer, mead, or wine. The quality of this beverage is average, and it is perfectly drinkable. If poisons, disease, or other toxins are removed as part of this spell, they make the drink darker and more full-bodied.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: none, Spell Resistance: yes
