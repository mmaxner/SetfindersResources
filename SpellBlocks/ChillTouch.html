Chill Touch
1
Casting Time: Standard Action
Target: Creature(s) touched
Components: V, S
1 touch/level deals 1d6 cold damage and must make a Fortitude save or lose 1 STR. Undead take no damage, but must make a will save or be Panicked for 1d4 +1 rounds.
Range: touch
Saving Throw: Fort partial, Spell Resistance: Yes
